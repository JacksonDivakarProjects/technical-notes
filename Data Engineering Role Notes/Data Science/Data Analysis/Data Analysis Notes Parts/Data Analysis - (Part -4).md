# Part 4: Industry-Specific Cheat Sheets
*Immediate Actions for Your Business Type*

## Retail/E-commerce

### Daily Dashboard (5-Minute Check)
```
🛒 Conversion Rate: ___% (Goal: >2%)
💰 Average Order Value: $___ (Goal: Increase 5% monthly)  
📦 Units per Transaction: ___ (Goal: >1.5)
🛍️ Cart Abandonment Rate: ___% (Goal: <60%)
```

### Weekly Deep Dives

**Product Performance:**
- Fastest moving items (reorder immediately if low stock)
- Slowest moving items (mark down or bundle)
- Out-of-stock items (calculate lost revenue)

**Customer Insights:**
- New vs returning customer ratio
- Top geographic areas for sales
- Customer acquisition cost by channel (Google/FB/Email)

**Immediate Actions:**
1. **If conversion rate drops:** Check site speed, simplify checkout process
2. **If cart abandonment spikes:** Add exit-intent popups, offer free shipping
3. **If certain products stagnate:** Create bundles or flash sales
4. **If shipping costs rise:** Implement minimum order value for free shipping

### Critical Metrics to Track Weekly:
- Inventory turnover rate
- Return rate by product
- Customer lifetime value
- Marketing ROI by channel

## SaaS/Software Business

### Daily Health Check
```
📊 Daily Active Users: ___ (Look for drops)
🔄 Trial Signups: ___ (Goal: Steady or growing)
💸 New MRR: $___ (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
❌ Churn: ___ customers (Investigate immediately if spike)
```

### Weekly Analysis

**Product Usage:**
- Most used features (double down on these)
- Least used features (consider removing or improving)
- User drop-off points in onboarding

**Customer Health:**
- Customers approaching renewal
- Support ticket volume and response times
- Feature requests by segment

**Financial Health:**
- MRR growth rate
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Lifetime value (LTV) to CAC ratio (Goal: >3:1)

**Immediate Actions:**
1. **If churn increases:** Personal outreach to cancelled customers
2. **If trial conversion drops:** Simplify onboarding process
3. **If support tickets spike:** Create knowledge base articles
4. **If feature usage declines:** Send targeted tutorials

### Critical Metrics:
- Monthly churn rate (Goal: <2%)
- Trial-to-paid conversion rate (Goal: >25%)
- Net Revenue Retention (Goal: >100%)
- Customer support satisfaction score

## Service Business (Agencies, Consulting, Contractors)

### Weekly Performance Snapshot
```
⏱️ Billable Utilization: ___% (Goal: >75%)
📅 Project Profitability: ___% (Track by client/project)
👥 Client Retention Rate: ___% (Goal: >90%)
💰 Revenue per Employee: $___ (Measure efficiency)
```

### Monthly Deep Dives

**Client Profitability:**
- Most profitable clients (give them white-glove service)
- Least profitable clients (raise prices or transition out)
- Client acquisition cost vs lifetime value

**Service Delivery:**
- Project timeline adherence
- Team capacity vs demand
- Service quality metrics

**Financial Health:**
- Accounts receivable aging
- Profit margins by service type
- Cash flow projections

**Immediate Actions:**
1. **If utilization drops:** Adjust staffing or increase sales efforts
2. **If project margins shrink:** Review scope creep and change orders
3. **If client satisfaction dips:** Implement regular check-ins
4. **If cash flow tightens:** Invoice more frequently, require deposits

### Critical Metrics:
- Realization rate (billable hours vs actual worked)
- Client satisfaction score
- Project completion rate on time
- Revenue growth from existing clients

## Restaurant/Food Business

### Daily Operations Check
```
🍽️ Table Turnover: ___ times (Lunch vs Dinner)
👥 Covers per Hour: ___ (Peak vs Slow periods)
💰 Average Spend per Cover: $___
📱 Takeout/Delivery: ___% of total sales
```

### Weekly Analysis

**Menu Performance:**
- Best selling items (ensure never out of stock)
- Lowest selling items (consider removing)
- Food cost percentage by item (Goal: <30%)
- Profit margin by menu section

**Customer Patterns:**
- Peak days/hours (optimize staffing)
- Popular modifications or special requests
- Repeat customer rate

**Operational Efficiency:**
- Food waste percentage
- Labor cost percentage (Goal: <25%)
- Inventory turnover

**Immediate Actions:**
1. **If food costs rise:** Renegotiate with suppliers, adjust portion sizes
2. **If table turnover slows:** Train staff on service timing
3. **If certain items don't sell:** Create specials or remove from menu
4. **If takeout grows:** Optimize packaging and delivery process

### Critical Metrics:
- Prime cost (food + labor) as % of sales (Goal: <60%)
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Employee turnover rate
- Social media engagement and reviews

- Capitalization rate
- Tenant lifetime value

## The Universal 30-Minute Weekly Review
*For Any Business Type*

**Every Monday Morning:**
1. **Last Week's Numbers** (10 min)
   - Sales/revenue vs target
   - Key metric performance
   - Any red flags from daily checks

2. **Customer Insights** (10 min)
   - New customer sources
   - Customer feedback trends
   - Retention/churn patterns

3. **Action Plan** (10 min)
   - One thing to start doing
   - One thing to stop doing  
   - One thing to investigate further

**Remember:** The goal isn't perfect analysis. The goal is **good enough data to make better decisions than your competitors.**

You now have everything you need to stop guessing and start making data-driven decisions in your business.

**Start this week with your industry cheat sheet. Pick one metric to track daily and one analysis to complete weekly. The rest will follow naturally.**
