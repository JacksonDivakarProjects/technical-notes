Jasseur and Filali (2023) proposed a real-time people counting system capable of estimating age and gender using a deep learning–based multi-object tracking framework built on FairMOT. Their system assigns persistent identities to individuals and performs demographic estimation even in crowded environments, achieving real-time performance with reliable accuracy. However, the proposed approach is limited to perception-level analytics and does not extend to downstream business intelligence, KPI computation, or decision support.
