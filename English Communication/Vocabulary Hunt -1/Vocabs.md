**“Synergy”** is a **noun** used in business and professional contexts to describe the **combined effect of a group or partnership that is greater than the sum of individual efforts**.

---

### 🔹 Meaning

- **When two or more entities work together and produce a better result than they could alone**.  
    → _Example:_ “The merger created synergy between the marketing and sales teams.”  
    _(Meaning: working together generated better outcomes than separately.)_
    

---

### 🔹 Synonyms

- Cooperation
    
- Collaboration
    
- Teamwork
    
- Partnership
    
- Alliance
    

### 🔹 Antonyms

- Conflict
    
- Disunity
    
- Opposition
    
- Rivalry
